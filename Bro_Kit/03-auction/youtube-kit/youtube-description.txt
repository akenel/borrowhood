Auction -- Competitive Bidding with Live Countdown | BorrowHood EP3

Dave lists his Mountain Bike with a 2-minute countdown. Sally bids EUR 50. Rosa outbids at EUR 75. Sally comes back at EUR 130 -- reserve price met. The timer hits zero. Sally wins the bike.

Real auction. Real countdown. Real bidding war. No fake demos.

WHAT YOU'RE SEEING
- Owner creates auction listing with starting bid, reserve price, and bid increment
- 2-minute live countdown timer (real, not simulated)
- Bidders place bids through the item detail page
- Each bid updates the current price and bid count in real time
- Reserve price indicator shows when the minimum has been met
- Anti-snipe protection extends the timer if bids come in the final seconds
- Owner ends auction after timer expires -- winner and amount confirmed
- Three users, three sessions, one auction -- all through the browser

AUCTION RULES
- Starting bid: EUR 50
- Bid increment: EUR 5
- Reserve price: EUR 120 (hidden from bidders)
- Duration: 2 minutes
- Winner: highest bidder when timer hits zero (if reserve met)

TECH STACK
- FastAPI + SQLAlchemy (async)
- Alpine.js (reactive auction panel, live countdown)
- Tailwind CSS
- Keycloak OIDC (session-based auth)
- PostgreSQL
- Docker Compose on Hetzner VPS
