**Leonardo grabs the bike for parts. Gets an idea on the walk home.**

4. Leonardo character overlay on homepage (legend, moderator, "the original maker")

8. Leo picks up the bike at Johnny's place.
9. Card: JOHNNY'S STORY -- Johnny listed it because the scrap yard is across town. Half a day carrying it on his shoulder for pennies. He still loves this bike. Doesn't want it melted down. Hopes someone on BorrowHood does something with it. Johnny tells Leo the story. Leo listens. Feels the kid's pain. Says nothing.
10. Leo leaves with the bike on his shoulder. Johnny says goodbye to his Bianchi.
11. Leo is walking. Carrying the bike. Feeling the frame. His maker brain kicks in: "Wait. The frame is cracked but... this is a 5-minute weld job. I just need a welder."
12. Leo pulls out his phone on the street. Browses BorrowHood. Mike's Garage -- 3-hour welding course, EUR 30. The cracked frame is the perfect training project. And he learns to weld too.
13. Mike character overlay (pillar, "If it's broken, I can fix it")
14. Leo books Mike's training. (PLATFORM TRANSACTION #1 -- EUR 30). Keeps walking to Mike's Garage. It's on the way home.

### ACT V -- THE WELD (Scenes 31-36)

**Mike teaches. Leo learns. The bike is reborn.**

31. Card: AFTER LUNCH -- Mike's Garage
32. Mike's workshop -- MIG 200A, garage bay, tools everywhere.
33. Mike does a 5-second stitch weld demo on scrap metal. Torch, puddle, bead. "That's all there is to it."
34. Leo: "Got it." Takes the welder. Welds the cracked Bianchi frame. First weld. Invisible. Perfect.
35. Mike: "How long have you been welding?!" Leo: "How long did that take?" They both laugh.
36. Mike: "Bro, you still want the 3-hour course? You don't need it. I can't charge you for 5 minutes." Mike gives the full EUR 30 refund on BorrowHood -- service paid, not fully used. (PLATFORM REFUND -- shows the return flow)
37. Mike looks up Leo on BorrowHood right there in the garage. Sees the profile: LEGEND badge. 1400 points. 45 reviews. Moderator. Mike: "Whoa. You really are the real McCoy." Mike FAVORITES Leo. Browses Leonardo's Bottega offerings. The wheels start turning in Mike's head.
38. Overlay: THE GARAGE TALK -- While the weld cools, Mike and Leo talk shop. Mike says he only lists some of his stuff on BorrowHood. He's got a wood chipper, a concrete saw, heavy power tools -- but he's afraid someone will get hurt and he'll be liable. Leo the legal beagle: "Have them sign a rental agreement. Rent at your own risk. There are special insurances for exactly this. If they hurt themselves, that's on them -- as long as you've got the paperwork." Mike didn't know that. Leo just unlocked 20 more listings for Mike's Garage.
39. Card: TWO TEACHERS -- Mike taught Leo to weld in 5 seconds. Leo taught Mike about liability in 5 minutes. Mike made EUR 0 today but gained a legend as a favorite and 20 new listings to post. The wheels are turning. Mike wants to learn more from this guy -- legal, business, everything.
37. Back at the Bottega -- Leo replaces the sprocket from his own stock. New chain. Tunes gears. That's why the chain kept snapping -- bad sprocket all along.
38. The bike is reborn.

    - Johnny is out delivering on Sally's scooter. Making EUR 55 a day.
    - He thinks his bike is scrap metal in someone's garage.
    - He doesn't know Leo fixed it.
    - He doesn't know it's better than new.
    - **"What happens when a legend... fixes what everyone said was scrap?"**
