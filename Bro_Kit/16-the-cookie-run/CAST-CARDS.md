**The never-ending story:** Every transaction creates a relationship. Every relationship creates an opportunity. Every opportunity creates a transaction. The circle never closes.

## Help Board Subplot (EP3)

The Help Board is where strangers become neighbors:

1. Sofia posts: "Need last-minute cookie deliveries across Trapani!"
2. Nino replies: "I can help. EUR 8/delivery. But I need wheels."
3. Sally replies: "Rent my scooter. EUR 5/day. EUR 50 deposit."
4. Nino calculates: 3 deliveries x EUR 8 = EUR 24 - EUR 5 scooter = EUR 19 profit
5. Nino rents scooter, does deliveries
6. Nino tries wheelies, crashes scooter
7. Sally: "Damage needs assessment"
8. Leo (on Help Board): "Bring it to my place, I'll look at it"
9. Mike (on Help Board): "I can fix anything, at a cost"
10. Scooter gets fixed, Nino pays for damage, lesson learned
