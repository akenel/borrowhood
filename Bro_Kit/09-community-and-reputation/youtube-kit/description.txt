What happens after the rental is done? This video goes beyond borrowing and returning. It shows the community features that make BorrowHood a real neighborhood -- not just a rental platform. Reviews, badges, member discovery, favorites, and a helpboard where neighbors help neighbors.

REVIEWS -- SAYING THANKS
Mike just finished a rental with Sally. She returned his drill in perfect condition. He opens BorrowHood and leaves her a 5-star review with a note: "Sally is the kind of neighbor you want." The review appears on her profile instantly. Her review summary updates. Trust is building.

BADGES -- EARNING REPUTATION
Every action in BorrowHood earns points. Points unlock badges. After leaving the review, Mike checks his profile and earns a new badge. There are 14 badges total: First Listing, First Rental, Trusted Renter, Community Voice, Neighborhood Hero, and more. Your badge tier (Newcomer, Active, Trusted, Pillar, Legend) determines the weight of your reviews.

NOTIFICATIONS -- STAYING IN THE LOOP
Sally logs in and sees a notification on the bell icon. "Mike left you a 5-star review." She opens it, reads it, marks it as read. Every important event -- reviews, badge earned, rental updates -- triggers a notification. No surprises.

MEMBERS DIRECTORY -- DISCOVERING NEIGHBORS
Sally opens the Members page. She can filter by badge tier, city, workshop type, or search by name. She filters for "Trusted" members and discovers Maria's Garden -- a neighbor she has never met. She favorites Maria with a single click. Then she visits Maria's workshop page and sees her items, skills, languages, and reviews.

HELPBOARD -- NEIGHBORS HELPING NEIGHBORS
Not everything is about renting. Sally needs to hang floating shelves but does not know which wall anchors work on old plaster walls. She posts a need on the Helpboard: "Toggle bolts? Molly bolts? Anyone dealt with this?" Maria sees the post and replies: "Toggle bolts are the way to go! I hung my cabinets with them two years ago." Sally reads the reply and marks the post as resolved. Problem solved. No transaction. Just neighbors.

WEIGHTED REPUTATION
Not all reviews are equal. A review from a Legend-tier member carries 10x the weight of a Newcomer. This means you cannot game the system with fake accounts. Real reputation comes from real participation over time.
