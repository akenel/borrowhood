# What's New on La Piazza

**Week of April 19–25, 2026**

A *busy* week. We shipped polish across the whole platform — and made the small things matter.

---

## ✨ New Things You Can Do

- **Type-aware Listing Flow** — Land on `/list?type=event` (or raffle, or workshop) and the form already knows what you're posting. Less clicking, more sharing.
- **Calendar that Filters** — Pick *Mine*, *Free*, or *Paid* and your view sticks. Empty state suggests where to look next instead of just showing nothing.
- **8-Box Lockbox Codes** — Picking up or returning a rental? The code entry is now an 8-box OTP-style input. Same flow, no more squinting.
- **Per-Item Analytics** on your dashboard — see views (last 30 days), favorites, and conversations on each of your items. Know what's working.
- **Redesigned My Items Cards** — bigger thumbs, color-coded type badges, status pills, View / Edit / Delete one tap away. Mobile-first.
- **Attendee Notes for RSVPs** — let your guests tell you about food allergies, plus-ones, or what they're bringing.
- **Free-Text Availability** on listings — say "weekends only" or "after 6pm" without a calendar picker.
- **Raffles in the Listing Flow** — list a raffle alongside items and events.
- **Reward Loop for Feedback** — when you report a bug or share an idea, you earn points and contributor badges.
- **Software Legends Seed** — six founding-developer profiles (antirez, Carmack, DHH, Linus, Bellard, Acton) live on La Piazza as inspiration.

## 🔧 Polish & Better UX

- **Mobile overflow squashed** — input rows wrap properly, long words break, no more horizontal scroll on narrow screens.
- **Cleaner Mobile Nav** — every menu item now has an icon (was just Raffles + Settings).
- **Auto-translate popup gone** — your browser stops offering "Show Original" on already-localized pages.
- **Calendar Tabs as Dropdown** on mobile — saves space.
- **Tap-to-Cover** for item photos — pick which image is the hero with one tap.
- **Every Tag is Clickable** on item details, profiles, and sidebars — discover everything tied to "boxing" or "ceramica" instantly.
- **Smart Empty States** across search and browse — echo your query, suggest where else to look.
- **Raffle-Aesthetic Item Cards** rolled out across browse, home, and members.
- **Heart + Share Channels** on event cards — favorite an event or share it to WhatsApp, Telegram, etc.
- **Event Cards Don't Cram Titles** to "M…" anymore on mobile.

## 🐛 Bugs Squashed

- **Lockbox Verify** would sometimes leave a stuck "Verifying…" spinner — three layers of defense added (15s timeout, 20s watchdog, immediate reload on success).
- **Confirm Pickup/Return** no longer shows up before the codes exist — you'll see a "Waiting for seller" hint instead.
- **Feedback Photo Uploads** — Sylvie's bug: a 12 MB photo of Simon was silently rejected because the error said "max 10 MB" but the real limit was 25 MB. Fixed: server tells the truth, client compresses iteratively, HEIC photos are detected and explained.
- **Listing Edits No Longer Wipe** your story, tags, age restrictions, or safety notes when you save.
- **Raffle Creation MissingGreenlet** error — fixed.
- **Calendar Event Images** now render reliably.

## 🛡️ Trust & Safety

- **Raffle Trust Gate** tightened — Simon (and any new account) capped at €10 pots, age-14 floor enforced, broken raffle validation rejected.
- **Live Raffle Events** — full design doc and onboarding kit shipped, ready for the first in-person night.
- **Sold-To Visible** on seller's order view — no more guessing who bought your item.
- **Lockbox Audience Hints** — codes block clearly labels who the code is for.
