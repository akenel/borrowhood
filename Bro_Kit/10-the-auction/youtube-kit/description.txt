What happens when two neighbors want the same item? They bid for it. This video shows the full auction system in BorrowHood -- live bidding, reserve prices, countdown timer, and anti-snipe protection. Every feature explained so clearly that even a five-year-old could follow along.

MEET THE DRONE
Jake runs Jake's Tech Bench and has a DJI Mini 3 Pro in like-new condition. Under 249g, so no licence needed in Italy -- perfect for beginners. 4K/60fps video, 48MP photos, obstacle avoidance. Instead of setting a fixed price, he creates an auction listing. Starting bid: EUR 80. Reserve price: EUR 200 (hidden from bidders). Bid increment: EUR 10. The 2.5-minute countdown starts. Winner gets a free 15-minute orientation flight at pickup -- because neighbors look out for each other.

WHAT IS AN AUCTION?
You put something up for sale. People bid money. Each bid must be higher than the last. A countdown timer ticks down. When the timer hits zero, the highest bidder wins. That is the whole thing.

SALLY BIDS FIRST
Sally wants the drone for aerial food photography. She places the opening bid at EUR 80. She is the current high bidder. But the reserve price is NOT met -- even if nobody else bids, Jake does not have to sell. The price is too low.

MIKE OUTBIDS
Mike spots the auction. He wants the drone for roof inspections at his garage. He bids EUR 150. Sally gets an "outbid" notification instantly. The reserve is still not met.

THE RESERVE PRICE
A secret minimum price set by the owner. If the highest bid stays below EUR 200, Jake does not have to sell. If it reaches EUR 200 or above, the sale is guaranteed. A safety net: "I will sell, but only if the price is right."

SALLY GOES ALL IN
Sally is not giving up. She comes back with EUR 250 -- well above the EUR 200 reserve. The panel shows a green "Reserve Met" badge. The sale is now guaranteed. Mike would need to go even higher.

ANTI-SNIPE PROTECTION
What if someone places a bid in the last 60 seconds? The system adds 2 more minutes to the timer. This gives everyone a fair chance to respond. No more "sniping" -- no sneaky last-second bids that nobody can react to.

THE COUNTDOWN
Jake watches the timer tick down to zero. 3 bids placed. Reserve met. EUR 250 on the table. The clock hits zero.

AUCTION ENDS -- SALLY WINS
Jake ends the auction officially. Sally wins the DJI Mini 3 Pro at EUR 250. Her bid is marked as WON. She gets a notification: "You won the auction!" Jake gets a notification with the final bid count and winning amount. Sally picks up the drone and gets her free orientation flight from Jake.

FEATURE CHECKLIST
- Live bidding in real time
- Starting bid (minimum price to enter)
- Bid increment (must bid at least EUR 10 more each time)
- Reserve price (secret minimum, protects the owner)
- Live countdown timer
- Outbid notifications (know instantly when someone beats you)
- Anti-snipe protection (last-minute bids extend the timer)
- Winner announced when the timer hits zero
