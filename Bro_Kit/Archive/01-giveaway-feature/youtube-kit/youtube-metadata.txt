TITLE:
BorrowHood Giveaway Feature -- Claim Free Items From Your Neighbors

DESCRIPTION:
People have stuff in their attic they want to give away for free. Pick it up and it's yours.

We built a dedicated Giveaway feature for BorrowHood -- a community sharing platform running on FastAPI + Keycloak.

What you'll see in under 2 minutes:
- Browse page with green "Give Away" badges and FREE labels
- Item detail with "Claim This Item" button (no dates, no deposits)
- Sally claims Mike's old garden hose with a message
- Mike approves the claim -- giveaway auto-completes, listing pauses
- "Give Away" option on the List Item form (hides price fields)
- Full Italian localization ("Regalo" = Give Away)

The entire claim flow: see it, claim it, owner approves, go pick it up. Done.

Behind the scenes:
- New GIVEAWAY listing type (separate from rentals and offers)
- Simplified claim modal -- just a message, no date pickers
- Auto-complete on approve (skips pickup/return state machine)
- Listing auto-pauses after claim (one item, one claimant)
- "Generous Neighbor" badge at 3+ giveaways (25 points each)
- EN + IT localization

Built with Claude Code (AI co-pilot). Recorded with Puppeteer + OBS.

Tech stack: FastAPI, SQLAlchemy, Jinja2, Tailwind CSS, Alpine.js, Keycloak OIDC, Docker, Hetzner Cloud.
