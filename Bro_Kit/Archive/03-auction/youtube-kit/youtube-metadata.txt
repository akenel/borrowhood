TITLE:
BorrowHood Auction -- Competitive Bidding with Anti-Snipe Protection

DESCRIPTION:
The full auction system. Bid, outbid, reserve met, winner announced. Every click is real.

Dave lists his Specialized Rockhopper Mountain Bike as an auction. Sally and Rosa bid against each other. Reserve price, countdown timer, outbid notifications -- the whole thing live on a Hetzner server.

What makes this different:
- Live countdown timer with days, hours, minutes, seconds
- Outbid notifications sent to previous winning bidder
- Reserve price -- hidden minimum the seller will accept
- Anti-snipe protection -- last-second bids extend the auction by 2 minutes
- Bid increment validation -- each bid must be EUR 5 higher
- Owner can end the auction early once reserve is met

Built with Claude Code (AI co-pilot). Recorded with Puppeteer + OBS.

Tech stack: FastAPI, SQLAlchemy, Jinja2, Tailwind CSS, Alpine.js, Keycloak OIDC, Docker, Hetzner Cloud.
