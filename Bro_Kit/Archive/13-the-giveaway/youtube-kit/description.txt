BorrowHood introduces giveaways. Free items, one click to claim, auto-complete on approve.

This episode demonstrates the full giveaway flow: browse FREE items with green badges, claim with a message, owner approves with one click, listing auto-completes and pauses. No pricing, no deposits, no return dates. Just generosity.

Featuring: Mike's Garden Hose, Sally's Bread Maker, Rosa's Kids Bicycle, and Grace Hopper's Nanosecond Wire teaching aid.

No voiceover. Just the feature, live on Hetzner.

How giveaways work:
- Owner lists an item as "Give Away" (no price field)
- Browse page shows green FREE badge
- Claimer sees green "Claim This Item" button (no date picker)
- Claimer writes a message explaining why they want it
- Owner approves -- rental auto-completes immediately
- Listing auto-pauses (item is gone)
- 25 points per giveaway (generosity valued)
- "Generous Neighbor" badge after 3+ giveaways

Stack: FastAPI, SQLAlchemy, PostgreSQL, Jinja2, Alpine.js, Tailwind CSS.
