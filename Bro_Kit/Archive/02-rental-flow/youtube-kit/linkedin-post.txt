Sally needs a drill. Mike has one in his garage.

How do you handle the handoff without either of them being home at the same time?

Lock box codes.

We just shipped the full rental flow for BorrowHood -- a community sharing platform we're building in Sicily.

Here is how it works:
-- Sally finds the drill on Browse (EUR 8/day + EUR 30 deposit)
-- She picks dates, types a message, hits "Rent This"
-- Mike sees the request, clicks Approve
-- Mike types "garage side door, key under flower pot" and generates lock box codes
-- Sally sees the pickup code on her dashboard, goes to the garage, grabs the drill
-- She clicks "I've Picked It Up" -- status updates to PICKED_UP
-- When she is done, she returns it and clicks "I've Returned It"
-- Mike clicks "Complete" -- both earn reputation points

No meetup scheduling. No missed calls. No "are you home?" texts.

One pickup code. One return code. One-time use each. The whole state machine runs in 5 steps:
PENDING to APPROVED to PICKED_UP to RETURNED to COMPLETED.

Watch the 3-minute demo. Every click is real, running on a live Hetzner server.
