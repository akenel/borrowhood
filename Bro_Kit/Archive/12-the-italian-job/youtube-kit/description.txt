No voiceover. Just the feature, live on Hetzner.

Stack: FastAPI, Jinja2 i18n, JSON locale files (en.json / it.json), cookie-based language preference, Alpine.js reactive UI.

547 translation keys covering:
- Navigation and menus
- Item categories and subcategories
- Badge tiers (Newcomer to Legend)
- Form labels and validation messages
- Status badges and filters
- Help board and community features
