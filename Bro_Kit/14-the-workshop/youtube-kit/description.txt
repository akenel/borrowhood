How workshops work:
- Every user has a workshop profile (name, type, tagline, bio)
- Add skills with self-rating (1-5 stars)
- Neighbors verify your skills (trusted expert at 5+ verifications)
- List services: "I'll fix your furniture" with hourly/flat pricing
- Customers request quotes describing what they need
- Provider responds with labor hours, materials cost, total
- Customer accepts or declines within 7 days
- Service quotes track the full lifecycle: requested to completed

Account deletion:
- Dashboard Settings has a Danger Zone section
- Safety gates block deletion if you have active rentals, held deposits, or open disputes
- Soft-delete hides profile, pauses listings, clears notifications
- GDPR-ready: your data, your choice

Stack: FastAPI, SQLAlchemy, PostgreSQL, Jinja2, Alpine.js, Tailwind CSS.
