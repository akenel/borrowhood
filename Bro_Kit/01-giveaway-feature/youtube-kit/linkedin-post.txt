People have stuff in their attic they want to give away for free.

Why make it complicated?

We just shipped a Giveaway feature for BorrowHood -- a community sharing platform we're building in Sicily.

The flow is dead simple:
-- List your item as "Give Away" (price fields disappear)
-- Neighbor sees it on browse with a green FREE badge
-- They click "Claim This Item" and send a message
-- You approve. They pick it up. Done.

No pricing. No deposits. No return dates. No state machine gymnastics.

One approval click and the giveaway auto-completes. Listing pauses. Item has a new home.

We even added a "Generous Neighbor" badge for people who give away 3+ items. Generosity should be visible.

The whole feature -- backend, frontend, claim flow, auto-complete, badge system, EN + IT localization -- built and deployed in one session with Claude Code as co-pilot.

Watch the 2-minute demo. Every click is real, running on a live Hetzner server.
